    HackJos

    Hackjos is nHub’s Flagship Challenge-Driven Conference and Hackathon that brings together leading industry players within and outside the country to share their candid and powerful experiences centered on entrepreneurship, innovation, best practices, brainstorming solutions and networking.The core objectives of HackJos include among others: creating opportunities for young people to explore the exciting world of entrepreneurism and software/apps development.

    Contact Us

  • Our Events

    Innov8Jos

    Innov8Jos is startup competition that is aimed at raising seed funding for budding entrepreneurs and mid-sized businesses in growth mode seeking investment in Plateau State. The competition involves a pitch session where startups that have been pre-screened will get to pitch their startups ideas to a panel of judges and the most innovative ideas goes home with prizes and some also securing a place in nHub incubation and acceleration program.
